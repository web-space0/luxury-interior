Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take on international projects?

Yes, we serve high-net-worth clients globally. While our primary design studio operations are centralized, our team frequently travels to oversee international estate builds, bespoke material sourcing in Europe, and final turnkey styling.

What is your minimum project scope?

We specialize in comprehensive design solutions. To ensure the level of uncompromising quality and cohesion we are known for, we typically undertake full-home, penthouse, or extensive multi-room renovations rather than single-room styling.

How does the custom furniture curation work?

We leverage our exclusive relationships with master artisans and luxury brands across Italy, France, and globally. We source, customize, and occasionally commission entirely unique pieces tailored to the specific dimensions and aesthetic of your space.
